SNT land

A place for those looking for a Success Networking Team (SNT). Whether you wish to create your own SNT or join another, you are one step closer to becoming inducted.

Success Networking Teams are usually created during your Leadership Training Day (LTD) event. These teams are made up of a minimum of 3 people and a maximum of 7 people. Your members will be the glue that keeps you together while trying to achieve the goals that you set yourself to attain. Whether you want to create or join a Success Networking Team, you will be able to do so here.
